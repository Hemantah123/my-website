About Me

Hello! I'm Hemanta Hazarika, a dedicated researcher specializing in Control Systems. Currently, I am pursuing my PhD at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay, where I focus on the intricate field of network dynamical descriptor systems. My research aims to enhance the understanding and application of control mechanisms within complex networked systems, which has far-reaching implications across various technological and engineering domains.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I am passionate about robotics, particularly in developing biologically inspired robots. One of my key interests lies in creating snake robots, which emulate the unique locomotion of real snakes. These robots have potential applications in search and rescue operations, medical procedures, and inspection tasks in hazardous environments, showcasing the intersection of biology and advanced robotics.

I am enthusiastic about collaborating with fellow researchers, industry professionals, and academic peers to further the development and application of advanced control systems and innovative robotic technologies.
